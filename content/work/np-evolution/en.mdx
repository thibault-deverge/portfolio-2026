# A career-coaching platform, from zero to production.

## Context

A career-coaching consultant was managing files, appointments and documents by hand. He needed
a platform structuring the whole journey, from first interview to final signature.

## Delivered

- Consultant and client management
- Interactive PDFs generated on the fly
- eIDAS-compliant electronic signatures

Design, development, database and deployment — solo.

## Stack

Next.js · TypeScript · Prisma · PostgreSQL · Vercel
